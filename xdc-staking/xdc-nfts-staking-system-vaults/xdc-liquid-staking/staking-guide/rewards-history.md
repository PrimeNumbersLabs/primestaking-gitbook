# Rewards History

Track every reward you've claimed. Each claim is recorded on-chain and displayed in the app.

---

### How to View

1. Go to [primestaking.xyz/xdc-liquid-staking](https://primestaking.xyz/xdc-liquid-staking/overview).
2. Open the **My Positions** or **Rewards** section.
3. Your full claim history is listed with dates and amounts.
