# How to Claim Rewards

Rewards accrue continuously at ~4.5% APY. You can claim them at any time - there is no lock-up or minimum waiting period.

---

### Steps

1. Go to [primestaking.xyz/xdc-liquid-staking](https://primestaking.xyz/xdc-liquid-staking/overview).
2. Open the **Rewards** tab.
3. Click **Claim**.
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Your rewards (paid in psXDC) will be sent to your wallet immediately upon confirmation.
